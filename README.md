n the digital age, easy access to inappropriate and forbidden (haram) content has 
become a serious problem for individuals and society, especially for the Muslim 
community that aims to uphold Islamic values. Existing digital filtering tools are often 
insufficient or do not provide complete guidance from an Islamic perspective. 
"Al-Hudud Companion" is a plan for an innovative digital protection tool that will use 
advanced AI/ML technology to identify and block haram, illegal, and inappropriate 
content, while also encouraging users towards good behavior through Islamic 
teachings and personal commitments. The goal of this tool is to provide an 
environment where Muslims can benefit from the digital world confidently without 
compromising their values.
